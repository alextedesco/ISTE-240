Gatlinburg Convention Center

The Gatlinburg Convention Center is a convention complex located in Gatlinburg, Tennessee, United States. It can hold 2,100 people in a theater setting and 1,050 in a banquet setting. The complex was recently renovated by Johnson and Galyon.

Hours


The Gatlinburg Convention is open daily from 10AM - 5PM.

Pricing (for ages 10 and up)

Visiting the Gatlinburg Convention Center is free for all!
