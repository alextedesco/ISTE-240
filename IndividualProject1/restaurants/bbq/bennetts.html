History

Bennett's Pit Bar-B-Que was founded in 1991 and is considered to be one of the best BBQ restaurants in Gatlinburg. Owned by the Johnson family, Bennett's recently won the 2022 Pidgeon Forge BBQ competition with its ribs, steak, and briskets blowing out the competition.

Reviews

Bennett's Pit Bar-B-Que currently has 4.5 stars out of 5 on Google Reviews on 4,174 reviews. Customers love its "large portion sizes" and very "knowledgeable, courteous, and helpful staff." The brisket, pulled pork, ribs, and signature sauce is among fan favorites at the establishment.
