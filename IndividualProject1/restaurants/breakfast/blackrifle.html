History

The Black Rifle Coffee Company was founded in 2014 by former U.S. Army Green Beret Evan Hafer. Their mission is to serve coffee and culture to people who love America and provide jobs and opportunities to veterans all over the United States. In July 2022 they partnered with the Dallas Cowboys and became their official distrubutor of coffee. They currently operate 18 stores and the Gatlinburg store can found at 1836 Parkway and contacted at (865) 286-5862.
