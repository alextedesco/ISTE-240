History

The Log Cabin Pancake House was opened in 1978 by the Huskey family. It is the longest running family owned restauant in the Gatlinburg/Pidgeon Forge area. In 2017 it relocated to a new location due to fears of more Gatlinburg fires putting risk to the restauant. It now resides on 327 Airport Rd and can be contacted at (865) 436-7894.

Reviews

The Log Cabin Pancake House currently has 4.7 stars out of 5 on Google Reviews on 5,294 reviews. Reviewers has said their "staff and service is incredible" and the breakfast is an "authentic and truly southern style with pancakes nice, flat, and almost crepe like".
