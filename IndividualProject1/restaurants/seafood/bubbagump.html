History

The Bubba Gump Shrimp Company was founded in 1995 after entrepreneur Anthony Zolezzi bought the rights to the name “Bubba Gump Shrimp Co.” from Paramount Pictures. Zolezzi did so in an attempt to turn around the financially struggling company Meridian Products. Jumping on the success of Forrest Gump worked amazingly for the company as now it is one of the largest seafood chains in America. They currently operate 34 stores and the Gatlinburg store can found at 900 Parkway and contacted at (865) 430-3034.

Reviews

The Bubba Gump Shrimp Company in Gatlinburg has 4.2 stars out of 5 on Google Reviews on 7,800 reviews. The restaurant is beloved for the Forrest Gump themes and quality seafood. The overall aesthetic and Forrest Gump references by the staff is what makes it a "prime place to visit".
