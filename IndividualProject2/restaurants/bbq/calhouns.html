History

Calhoun's was founded in 1983 with its first store in Kingston Pike. Their distinct and tasty BBQ methods have earned them the title of "Best Ribs in America", a slogan they still use today. Since opening their first store they have expanded to restaurants all over Tennessee bringing the award winning BBQ to the table. The Gatlinburg restaurant can be found at 1004 Parkway #101 and can be contacted at (865) 436-4100.

Reviews

Calhoun's in Gatlinburg currently has 4.2 stars out of 5 on Google Reviews on 4,106 reviews. Reviewers have said their signatures are the prime rib and steak being cooked to "perfection" and having "just the right amount of flavor with its BBQ sauce and an adequate amount of juiciness".
